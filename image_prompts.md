## After Generating

Once you generate the images, save them as:
- `assets/letter_d.png`
- `assets/letter_e.png`
- `assets/letter_f.png`
- ... and so on through `letter_z.png`

Then update `script.js` and change `hasImage: false` to `hasImage: true` for each letter you add.
